================================================================================
                         FINANCIAL DATA SEEDING
================================================================================

The seed function now creates comprehensive financial data:

BUDGET CATEGORIES (7 categories per project):
- Vifaa na Malighafi (35% of budget)
- Ajira na Mishahara (25% of budget)
- Usafirishaji na Logistiki (15% of budget)
- Matibabu na Bima (8% of budget)
- Uongozi wa Mradi (7% of budget)
- Mafunzo na Ujuzi (5% of budget)
- Mazingira na Usafi (5% of budget)

TRANSACTIONS:
- 5-15 transactions per budget category
- 3-8 general project transactions (not assigned to categories)
- Mix of transaction types: EXPENSE (80%), REFUND (8%), ADJUSTMENT (12%)
- Transactions spread over last 180 days
- Various approval statuses: APPROVED (85%), PENDING (10%), REJECTED (5%)
- Realistic Tanzanian vendors and descriptions

BUDGET ALERTS:
- Automatically created when budget utilization exceeds thresholds
- WARNING alerts at 85% utilization
- CRITICAL alerts at 95% utilization

SAVINGS RECORDS:
- 3 savings records per project (if estimated savings > 0)
- Various savings categories and reasons
- Verified savings with achievement dates

All financial calculations are automatically recalculated from transactions
to ensure accuracy.
